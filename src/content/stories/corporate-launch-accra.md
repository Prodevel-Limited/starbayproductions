## The Challenge

PanAfrican Banking Group was launching their revolutionary digital banking platform across West Africa. The high-stakes event would host 800 VIP guests including government officials, international investors, and media, with live national television broadcast. The venue—a premium hotel ballroom—presented acoustic challenges, and the presentation included complex multimedia elements requiring perfect synchronization.

The client's expectations were absolute: flawless audio for executive presentations, dramatic audio reveals for product demonstrations, and broadcast-quality sound for television coverage.

## Our Solution

Starbay Production designed and executed a sophisticated audio production:

- **Premium Sound Reinforcement**: Strategically positioned systems for even coverage without overwhelming the intimate space
- **Wireless Presentation Systems**: Discrete lavalier microphones allowing natural executive movement
- **Multi-Screen Synchronization**: Frame-accurate audio sync across six video displays
- **Broadcast Feed Management**: Dedicated mix meeting national television standards
- **Presentation Confidence Monitoring**: Discrete stage monitors for presenter awareness
- **Technical Rehearsal**: Full dress rehearsal with every presenter and video cue

## The Result

The launch proceeded without a single technical hesitation. Executive presentations were clear and confident, video demonstrations hit their marks perfectly, and the dramatic audio reveal of the new platform created the intended impact. Television broadcast quality exceeded the network's standards, leading to extended coverage.

The invisible technical excellence allowed the client's message—not the medium—to capture attention.
