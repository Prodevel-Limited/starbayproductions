## Strategic Technical Guidance

Not every project requires full production services. Sometimes you need expert guidance to optimize your existing setup, plan a major event, or train your team. Starbay Production offers comprehensive technical consultancy services that leverage our decades of experience to solve your unique challenges.

### Audio System Design & Installation

Permanent venue solutions:

- **Acoustic Analysis**: Room modeling and acoustic treatment recommendations
- **System Architecture**: Complete audio system design from source to speaker
- **Equipment Specification**: Vendor-neutral recommendations based on actual needs
- **Installation Oversight**: Quality control during installation phase
- **System Commissioning**: Calibration, tuning, and optimization
- **Documentation**: Complete system diagrams, settings, and operating procedures
- **Staff Training**: Hands-on instruction for your technical team

### Production Planning & Consultation

Strategic guidance for major events:

- **Technical Feasibility Studies**: Assessing what's possible for your vision and budget
- **Production Budgeting**: Realistic cost estimation and resource allocation
- **Vendor Coordination**: Managing multiple technical suppliers
- **Advance Planning**: Site surveys, load-in scheduling, and logistics
- **Risk Assessment**: Identifying potential technical challenges and mitigation strategies
- **Timeline Development**: Realistic production schedules
- **Technical Rider Creation**: Professional specifications for touring acts

### Equipment Configuration & Optimization

Get more from your existing gear:

- **Digital Mixer Programming**: Optimized scenes, routing, and recalls
- **Network Audio Setup**: Dante, AVB, or AES67 configuration
- **Wireless Frequency Coordination**: Maximize channel count and minimize interference
- **Signal Flow Optimization**: Efficient routing and gain staging
- **Effects Processing**: Reverb, delay, and dynamics configuration
- **Documentation**: Labeled block diagrams and configuration files
- **Training Sessions**: Teaching your team to use equipment effectively

### Venue Assessment & Recommendations

Professional evaluation for churches, theaters, and event spaces:

- **Existing System Audit**: Honest assessment of current capabilities
- **Upgrade Path Planning**: Phased improvement recommendations
- **Budget-Conscious Solutions**: Maximum impact for available resources
- **Future-Proofing**: Scalable systems that grow with your needs
- **Technology Integration**: Combining old and new equipment effectively
- **Acoustical Treatment**: Addressing room issues before throwing audio at them

### Remote Technical Support

Expert guidance without travel costs:

- **Virtual Consultations**: Video calls with screen sharing for troubleshooting
- **System Review**: Remote analysis of equipment lists and signal flow
- **Pre-Event Planning**: Technical guidance during event preparation
- **Real-Time Support**: Phone/video assistance during critical events
- **Post-Event Analysis**: Reviewing recordings and discussing improvements
- **Ongoing Retainer Services**: Regular check-ins and continuous support

### Training & Knowledge Transfer

Empower your team with professional expertise:

- **Operator Training**: Hands-on instruction for mixing consoles and processors
- **System Maintenance**: Proper care and troubleshooting techniques
- **Best Practices**: Professional workflows and industry standards
- **Troubleshooting Skills**: Systematic problem-solving approaches
- **Safety Protocols**: Electrical safety and rigging awareness
- **Custom Curriculum**: Training tailored to your specific equipment and needs

- **Budget Oversight**: Ensuring technical spending stays on track
- **Quality Assurance**: Verifying all deliverables meet specifications
