## Production Without Borders

When your event crosses international boundaries, the technical complexity multiplies. Different power standards, varied wireless frequency regulations, language barriers, customs requirements, and unfamiliar local suppliers create challenges that can derail even the most carefully planned productions. Starbay Production specializes in navigating these complexities, delivering consistent world-class audio regardless of location.

### International Touring Support

Seamless production for artists on the road:

- **Advance Coordination**: Pre-tour communication with venues across multiple countries
- **Technical Rider Negotiation**: Ensuring rider fulfillment in diverse markets
- **Local Crew Coordination**: Sourcing and managing qualified local technicians
- **Equipment Standardization**: Consistent gear package or local equivalent specification
- **Wireless Frequency Management**: Navigating different regulatory environments
- **Power & Signal Adaptation**: Managing voltage differences and connector standards
- **Backline Coordination**: Securing quality musical instruments and amplifiers
- **Rehearsal Space**: Identifying and preparing pre-show technical rehearsal locations

### Cross-Border Event Production

Managing events spanning multiple countries:

- **Language Support**: Multilingual technical communication
