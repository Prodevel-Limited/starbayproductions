## Audio Excellence for Digital Distribution

In today's connected world, your event doesn't end when attendees leave the venue. Starbay Production ensures that your content translates perfectly to digital platforms, delivering the same impact to online audiences as to those in the room.

### Live Streaming Audio

Broadcast-quality sound for virtual audiences:

- **Dedicated Streaming Mix**: Separate audio mix optimized for online platforms
- **Platform Integration**: Direct feed to YouTube, Facebook, Vimeo, and custom streaming services
- **Audio Encoding**: Proper bitrate and compression for clear, consistent streams
- **Multi-Camera Audio Sync**: Perfect synchronization across multiple video feeds
- **Backup Streaming**: Redundant paths ensure continuous broadcast
- **Real-Time Monitoring**: Dedicated engineer ensuring consistent online audio quality

### Multi-Track Recording

Capture every element for post-production flexibility:

- **Isolated Instrument Tracks**: Individual recording of all audio sources
- **Time-Stamped Session Files**: Professional DAW-compatible formats
- **Live Recording**: Simultaneous recording during events with zero disruption
- **Post-Production Ready**: Clean files ready for mixing and mastering
- **Archive Quality**: High-resolution audio for long-term preservation

### Broadcast Production Support

Professional audio for television and radio:

- **Broadcast Mix Creation**: Dedicated mix meeting broadcast standards
- **Truck Integration**: XLR/MADI/Dante feeds to production vehicles
- **Timecode Sync**: Frame-accurate synchronization with video
- **Communication Systems**: IFB and talkback for production coordination
- **Backup Feeds**: Redundant ISO outputs for broadcast safety
- **Standards Compliance**: EBU R128, ATSC A/85, or custom loudness specs

### Podcast & Content Capture

Studio-quality audio in live environments:

- **Isolated Speaker Recording**: Clean, broadcast-ready voice capture
- **Panel Discussion Recording**: Multi-microphone session recording
- **Audience Audio**: Separate crowd/ambience tracks
- **Quick Turnaround**: Same-day delivery of edited files when needed
- **Mobile Studio Setup**: Professional recording in any location

### Hybrid Event Production

Seamless integration of in-person and virtual experiences:

- **Dual Mix Philosophy**: Separate optimization for room and stream
- **Virtual Speaker Integration**: Clean audio from remote presenters
- **Interactive Audio**: Audience Q&A from both physical and virtual attendees
- **Platform-Specific Optimization**: Zoom, Teams, Webex, or custom platforms
- **Technical Coordination**: Managing the complexity so you don't have to

- **Wireless Microphone Coordination**: Frequency management with video crews
- **Reference Mix**: Headphone monitoring for videographers
- **Post-Production Files**: Synced audio files for editing
- **On-Set Communication**: Coordination with directors and camera operators
